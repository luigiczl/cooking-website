The definition of cooking

Cooking is the set of techniques for preparing food for human consumption, previously referred to as "cookery". Cooking is diverse throughout the world. It is one of the elements representing the notion of terroir when it is the fruit of natural resources and local agricultural production, habits and customs, culture and beliefs, the perfection of techniques, and exchanges between peoples and cultures.

Kitchen typology:
Each people, nation or civilisation has developed different cuisines based on local natural resources, but also on the character of each society, social evolution, traditions and interactions. The movement of peoples throughout history has led to mixtures and developments in cuisine.
